About Me

Technically curious.
Practically driven.

I'm a first-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida — and from day one, I've approached my degree as more than coursework. My focus is firmly on the domains that are reshaping the world: Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Robotics, and Embedded Systems.

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of intelligent software and physical systems — where an algorithm doesn't just run on a screen, but interacts with the real world. Whether it's training a model, analysing a dataset, or wiring up a microcontroller, my goal is always the same: build something that works and solves something that matters.

Beyond the technical side, I'm a disciplined learner, an engaged collaborator, and someone who genuinely enjoys the process of figuring things out. I'm actively seeking projects, communities, and challenges that push me to grow fast.
